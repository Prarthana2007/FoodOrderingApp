Food Ordering App Features:

1)Students and Admins have separate login.
2)College students can register their roll number and login.
3)Canteen names and location will be displayed.
4)Students can place their orders: add,remove,update with quantity.
5)Students can view the order id, price and status(Order confirmed,preparing,ready,pickedup).
6)Admin can update the food items available,view and confirm orders.
7)Once order is confirmed, payment is done and msg for successfull payment completion.
8)Students can choose pickup time, orders can be made preferably.
